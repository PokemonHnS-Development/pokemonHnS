Mime Jr -> Breed Mr. Mime with one parent holding Lax Incense

Happiny -> Breed Chansey or Blissey with one parent holding Lax Incense

Munchlax -> Breed Snorlax with one parent holding Lax Incense

Mantyke -> Breed Mantine with one parent holding Sea Incense
